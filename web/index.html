how it works

1
drop or pick your photos
one file or a whole batch — doesn't matter. jpg, png, webp, gif all work.
2
see what's in there
we scan the file and show you what metadata is embedded. GPS shows up in red because it's the one that actually matters.
3
strip and download
one click removes everything. download the clean version and share it wherever you want.
4
nothing leaves your device
all of this runs in your browser using the Canvas API. your photos never touch a server.
