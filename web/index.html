process

how it works

01
drop or pick your photos
one photo or a whole batch. jpg, png, webp, gif — all supported. drag straight onto the box or click to browse.
02
we scan what's hidden
GPS shows up in red. device model, editing software, timestamps — we surface everything buried in the file so you know what you're sharing.
03
strip and download clean
one click. all metadata gone. download the clean version — same image, nothing attached.
04
nothing leaves your device
this runs in your browser using the Canvas API. your photos are never sent anywhere. not to us, not to anyone.
