How it works

1
Drop or pick your photos
Select one photo or a whole batch. Supports JPG, PNG, WebP and more.
2
See what's hidden inside
We read the metadata and show you what's there — GPS location, device model, date taken, editing software.
3
Strip and download
One click removes everything. Download the clean photos ready to share anywhere.
4
Nothing leaves your device
All processing happens in your browser using Canvas API. No upload, no server, no account ever.
